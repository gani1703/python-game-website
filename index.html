WHAT IS IT

About the Game

This is a Python-based game where a human player competes against a computer opponent powered by smart logic. Test your skills, strategize, and try to beat the AI.

Human vs AI

Play against an intelligent computer opponent that adapts and challenges you every round.

Instant Feedback

Know your results in real-time. Every move is processed and displayed instantly.

Replayable

Play as many rounds as you want. Every game is different, every match a new challenge.
