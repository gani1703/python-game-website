About the Game

A modern, terminal-based Python game where you compete against a computer opponent. Choose your numbers (1-6) wisely. Test your predictive skills, strategize your batting, and chase down the target to beat the AI.

Human vs AI

Play against a computer opponent that unpredictably chooses its numbers to stump you.

Instant Feedback

Real-time terminal outputs calculating totals, targets, and match outcomes instantly.

Replayable

Every match is different. Win the toss, set massive targets, or defend small totals.

How to Play

1

The Toss

Launch the game. Choose ODD or EVEN and input a number (1-6) to decide who bats first.

2

Make Your Move

When batting or bowling, enter a number between 1 and 6 when prompted.

3

The Showdown

If your number matches the computer's number—the batter is OUT! Otherwise, the runs are added to the total score.

4

Victory Target

Chase down the set target when batting second, or defend your score by taking the computer's wicket.
